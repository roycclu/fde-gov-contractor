Why government contracting & legal?

Both markets are information-dense, time-sensitive, and historically underserved by modern search. GovWin costs $15K/year; Westlaw costs $5K+. Thousands of small firms and contractors navigate billion-dollar decision cycles using Google and PDFs. Exa's semantic retrieval — reading intent, not just keywords — closes that gap at a fraction of the price.
